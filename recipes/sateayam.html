Sate Ayam

Description

If you're a fan of bold, aromatic flavors, then sate ayam madura is the perfect dish for you. The marinade for the chicken is bursting with garlic, pepper, and lime juice, which infuses the meat with a tangy, savory taste. The peanut sauce is equally delicious, made with ground peanuts, spices, and sweet soy sauce that will have you licking your fingers in delight.

Ingredients

    CHICKEN MARINADE:

  • 400 grams diced chicken fillet
  • 1/2 lime, squeezed
  • 3 cloves of garlic, ground
  • 1/2 tsp ground pepper

    • PEANUT SAUCE:

  • 150 grams fried and ground peanuts
  • 3 sheets of torn orange leaves
  • 3 tbsp sweet soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp grated brown sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp granulated sugar
  • 500 ml water
  • 2 tbsp oil for sautéing

    • GROUND SPICES FOR PEANUT SAUCE:

  • 4 red curly chilies
  • 6 red onions
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 3 roasted candlenuts

    • SATE GLAZE:

  • 3 tbsp peanut sauce
  • 3 tbsp sweet soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp margarine

    • GARNISH:

  • Sliced red onions (as needed)
  • Green chilies (as needed)
  • Sweet soy sauce (as needed)
  • Diced lontong rice cake (as needed)

Steps

  1. Mix the ingredients for the chicken soak and marinade together and coat the diced chicken evenly. Place in the refrigerator and set aside.
  2. For the peanut sauce, Sauté the ground spices with the orange leaves until fragrant. Add the ground peanuts, brown sugar, soy sauce, sugar, and salt. Mix well. Pour in the water and cook until the sauce thickens and releases oil. Remove from heat and set aside.
  3. Remove the chicken from the refrigerator and skewer it.
  4. Prepare a grill/bbq/frying pan/flat non-stick pan.
  5. Mix the ingredients for the glaze and mix well. Apply the glaze to the skewers.
  6. Grill the skewers, turning and brushing them with the glaze, until cooked. Remove from heat.
  7. Serve with a drizzle of peanut sauce and the garnishes.
